Why We're Here

Watchers Circle is a public watchdog project. We provide a safe, anonymous space to report unethical or harmful behavior by those in positions of trust — whether they work in government, health care, housing, social services, education, or any community-serving role.

Our focus includes, but is not limited to:

  • Environmental or human rights violations
  • Abuse of power by public officials or professionals
  • Neglect or mistreatment of vulnerable populations
  • Corruption, coercion, fraud, or misconduct
  • Harmful exploitation of any kind

Submissions are verified and stored with care. Our mission is to build a circle of accountability — where no harm goes unseen.
