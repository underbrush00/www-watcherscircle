This form is 100% anonymous. No names, no logins, no tracking. You do not need to provide personal information.

Use this space to report concerns, harms, or misconduct that affect land, people, or community well-being — especially by those in positions of power. We welcome tips about:

  • Environmental violations
  • Exploitation of vulnerable people
  • Corruption within leadership or agencies
  • Misuse of funding or benefits
  • Violence, threats, or coercion

If known, include:

  • Location(s)
  • People or organizations involved
  • Dates or timelines
  • Any links, references, or evidence

All submissions are reviewed in private. If you want follow-up, include a ProtonMail address inside your message — but this is not required.
